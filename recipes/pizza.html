Pepperoni's piza

Description

Pepperoni Pizza is a classic and popular dish made with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and savory pepperoni slices. To prepare it, pizza dough is first rolled out into a round shape and placed on a baking pan. A layer of tomato sauce is spread evenly over the dough, followed by a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese. Pepperoni slices are then placed on top of the cheese, adding a spicy and smoky flavor. The pizza is baked in a hot oven until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese melts and bubbles. Once done, it is sliced and served hot, making it a delicious and comforting meal enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperonu slices
  • OLive oil
  • Dried oregano or italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to a high temperature (about 220°C / 425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a round shape on a baking pan or tray.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Place pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Add a light drizzle of olive oil and sprinkle seasoning if desired.
  7. Bake the pizza in the oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve hot.
