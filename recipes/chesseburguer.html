Chesseburguer

Description

A cheeseburger is a classic comfort food made with a juicy beef patty, melted cheese, and fresh toppings inside a soft bun. It is simple to prepare and loved for its rich, savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese slices (cheddar or American)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter or Oil

Steps

  1. Form the ground beef into patties.
  2. Season patties with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a pan or grill with oil.
  4. Cook patties on both sides.
  5. Add cheese on top and let it melt.
  6. Toast the burger buns.
  7. Assemble the burger with toppings.
  8. Serve warm.
