Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce

Description

Spaghetti with tomato sauce is a simple and delicious pasta dish made with long noodles and a flavorful tomato-based sauce. It is easy to cook and perfect for a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Dried basil or oregano
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook the spaghetti.
  2. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  4. Saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  5. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  7. Mix the pasta with the sauce.
  8. Serve hot with cheese if desired.
